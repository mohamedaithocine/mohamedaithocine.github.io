    An online and local party game of teamwork and betrayal for 4-15 players...in space! Complete tasks. Interrogate teammates, Vote out the imposter. Your goal is to survive. If you’re an imposter however, you have to sabotage the crew! Among Us is a multiplayer online game that has recently taken the gaming world by storm. Developed by InnerSloth, this game has quickly become one of the most popular games on the market. Its unique blend of strategy, mystery, and social interaction has made it a favourite among gamers of all ages.
